Information

QA Bug Reports is intended to help QA monitor the number of bugs discovered. The count can be incremented and decremented using the jolt arrows in the center of the page. Line graph reports for daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly bug statistics are available at the bottom of the page. Specific time frames can be searched within each report. The current number of sequential days without a customer complaint is displayed in the top right. This day streak can be reset with the red reset button.


Total Catastrophes Averted: All time cumulative count of bugs discovered.


Bugs Squished Today: Bug count for the current day.


Daily Reports: Daily reports are represented by 24 hours in a day.


Weekly Reports: Weekly reports are represented by days of the week.


Monthly Reports: Monthly reports are represented by 31 days in a month


Yearly Reports: Yearly reports is represented by 12 months in a year.
